About Us

Our Organization Story

Established in 2021, Utkal Foundation, founded by Trilochan Das, has been a leader in empowering marginalized communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable development initiatives. I founded Utkal Foundation driven by a profound commitment to creating positive change and responding to the pressing needs of our society. Witnessing the hardships faced by marginalized communities ignited my passion for social work and education, motivating me to take action. Through the foundation, I aim to empower individuals and communities by providing access to essential resources, education, and healthcare. Together with dedicated volunteers and supporters, we strive to break the cycle of poverty and uplift lives, fostering a sense of hope and opportunity for all.

Year of Establishment: 2021

Founded in 2021, Utkal Foundation is dedicated to uplifting marginalized communities through education, healthcare, and empowerment, working to create lasting change and improve lives.

Location: Bhadrak, Odisha

Based in Bhadrak, Odisha, Utkal Foundation serves local communities through education, healthcare, and empowerment programs, with a focus on reaching underserved areas.

Volunteer

Our volunteers play a vital role in delivering Education, Healthcare, and Empowerment programs to underserved communities. From organizing health camps to teaching children and supporting environmental initiatives, volunteers help us create lasting change on the ground. Every act of service brings us closer to building a more equitable society.

Global Reach

Utkal Foundation is committed to making a difference not just locally, but globally. Through our programs and partnerships, we extend support to marginalized communities worldwide, promoting education, healthcare, and sustainable development.

About Us

I founded the Utkal Foundation driven by a strong desire to create lasting, positive change in society and to address the critical needs of marginalized communities. My passion for social work and education grew from witnessing the deep struggles and inequities faced by those without access to essential resources. This inspired me to take action, not just to alleviate immediate challenges but to empower individuals and communities to build better, more sustainable futures. Through the Utkal Foundation, my goal is to create meaningful impact by providing opportunities that transform lives and foster a more just and equitable world.
