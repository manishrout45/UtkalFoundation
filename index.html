UTKAL FOUNDATION

(A Charitable Trust under Indian Trust Act- 1872)
We work on Education, Healthcare, Livelihood, Water Sanitation, Food, Security, Emergency, Economic Development, Climate Change, Agriculture and Disaster relief. We are working to keep unity and cooperation among the young mass of the community and to build moral life and character among the youth. Undertake intensive and extensive measures to organize people into Self Advocacy Groups (SAGs) at Village/Panchayat level, Federating them at block, district, state, and national levels in order to accelerate development. Support financially & guide them on how to succeed in various projects based on agriculture for SHG. We always work for the people in need.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

We are committed to uplifting lives by providing access to education, healthcare, and empowerment. We believe everyone deserves a fair chance to thrive, regardless of their circumstances. Our mission is to create lasting change by opening doors of opportunity for those in need. Together, we can build a brighter, more inclusive future.

Vision

We dream of a world where everyone has the chance to grow, learn, and lead a healthy, fulfilling life. A place where no one is left behind, and opportunities are available to all, regardless of where they c ome from. We strive to create lasting change that uplifts communities and ensures everyone can live with dignity and hope. Together, we can build a future where everyone has a fair shot at success.

Our Work

Education for All

We provide educational resources to children and adults in underserved communities, ensuring they have the tools to succeed in life.

Healthcare Support

Our healthcare camps offer free medical services to those who lack access to essential health facilities.

Environmental Sustainability

Our tree plantation drives and environmental awareness campaigns are aimed at protecting our planet for future generations.

Providing Relief in Times of Disaster

In times of disaster, we swiftly provide essential aid and support to help communities recover and rebuild their lives.

Anti-Smoking Campaigns

Our anti-smoking campaigns aim to raise awareness about the dangers of tobacco use and promote healthier, smoke-free lifestyles for individuals and communities.

Clean Water Access

We work to provide clean drinking water to communities, improving health and well-being.
